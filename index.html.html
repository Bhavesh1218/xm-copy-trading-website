Is my money safe with this service?+
Yes. Your funds are deposited in your own personal XM broker account. I only have copy trading permissions — I cannot withdraw, transfer, or access your capital in any way.
How do I receive my profit?+
Profits are credited directly to your XM account balance automatically. You can withdraw to your bank account or e-wallet via XM's withdrawal system at any time.
What happens if there is a loss?+
No performance fee is charged on losing trades. My 40% is only deducted when a trade is profitable. Losses are shared proportionally to your allocation in the copy portfolio.
Do I need any trading experience?+
None at all. Once your XM account is set up and linked to my profile, everything is fully automated. You don't need to understand charts, indicators, or markets.
Can I withdraw my money anytime?+
Yes. There is no lock-in period. You can stop copying and request a withdrawal at any time directly through XM's platform. Withdrawals are processed within 1–3 business days.
Is XM a reliable broker?+
Absolutely. XM is regulated by CySEC (EU), ASIC (Australia), and IFSC (Belize). They have been operating since 2009 and serve over 5 million clients across 190+ countries.
